## 💡 Pricing Psychology

### Why £2.99 and £4.99 Work:

1. **Charm Pricing**
   - .99 ending = perceived discount
   - Feels like "under £3" and "under £5"
   - Psychological price anchoring

2. **Coffee Test**
   - Lite = 1 coffee at Starbucks
   - Premium = 1-2 cocktails
   - Easy to justify monthly expense

3. **Competitor Comparison**
   - 50-70% cheaper than alternatives
   - Premium features at budget price
   - Clear value proposition

4. **Freemium Funnel**
   - Free → Lite: Low barrier (£2.99)
   - Lite → Premium: Small upgrade (+£2)
   - Easy to upsell
